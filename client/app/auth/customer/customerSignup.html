BarHawk is an app that calculates a customer's Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) throughout the night while improving the overall bar experience by streamlining ordering and fufillment.


BarHawk’s algorithm leverages the Windmark Formula and data collected throughout the night to dynamically calculate their BAC every 5 minutes. By providing a customer with their BAC they will be able to decide when it’s time to slow down or settle up.
